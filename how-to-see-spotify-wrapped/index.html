Frequently Asked Questions

When is Spotify Wrapped 2024 Available?

Spotify Wrapped 2024 typically becomes available in early December. The exact date varies each year, but users usually receive a notification when their Wrapped is ready. Data collection runs from January 1st to October 31st.

Why Can't I See My Spotify Wrapped?

  • • Ensure your Spotify app is updated to the latest version

How Is My Wrapped Data Calculated?

Spotify analyzes your listening history from January 1st to October 31st. The algorithm considers factors such as:

  • Total play time for songs, artists, and genres
  • Frequency of replays
  • Listening patterns across different times of day
  • Engagement with new releases and discoveries
